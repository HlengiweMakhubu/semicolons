HOW ARE WE: We are a group of young people from diverse cultural and geographic backgrounds who have come together with the goal of upskilling and enabling a better future for ourselves and our communities.

WHAT WE DID: We examined how to address mental health challenges in South Africa using design thinking principles. We created an innovative conceptual digital solution with the potential for social impact through a guided research and development approach based on new practices.

A community-based, digital healthcare channel for people with mental illnesses to bridge the gap between information, services, and lifestyle. The solution's goals are to: - satisfy people by providing them with qualified specialists - To provide up-to-date, relevant mental health information that is both enlightening and instructional. -To close the gap between users and necessary resources and services - Create user-based community advocates and ambassadors - Integrate self-care routines into daily life
